About This Event

Event Details

The Summer Music Festival is a celebration of music, art, and community. This all-day event features multiple stages with continuous performances from noon until midnight.

What to expect:

  • 12+ hours of live music
  • Food and beverage vendors
  • Art installations and interactive experiences
  • Merchandise booths
  • Chill-out zones

All ages are welcome. Children under 10 enter free with a paying adult.

The event will proceed rain or shine. In case of severe weather, announcements will be made regarding any schedule changes or safety procedures.
